# I am many things.

A writer, gamer, autist, artist, learner, coder, worldbuilder, christian, lover, in no particular order.

## Current Project

I've just started my education in the field of game and multimedia development and will be focusing on developing my skills with Photoshop, HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript and C# in the coming two years.

## Other Projects

I'm an ideas person - and since that line makes me cringe, I'm working on actually being able to work with my ideas, one way or another. Here's some of my projects I currently have going on.

### Worldbuilding: Yvari

I'm very excited about this pet project - [Yvari](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/yvari-imoenkim).

Yvari started as a crazy idea when it occured to me I wanted to try my hand on gamedev. If you want to learn it, what better way to do so on a personal project? And clearly you can't use an existing franchise, so I had to invent my own.

These days I spend a lot of time in the [Discord](https://discordapp.com/invite/WHuu4qH) belonging to the amazing community of [World Anvil]. The site facilitated the actual work on this crazy idea a lot, and without it and its community it would probably still be a crazy idea.

For now, I'm content with Yvari being all about stories, and I'm working on honing my writing skills on it. At some point, though, I hope to develop an RPG system for Yvari, and right now I'm debating whether to actually write a book or an adventure arc with the main story floating in my head.

### Jakand

A school project gone right, this is a tabletop game I've started developing with a team at school. Although the project has concluded, we are considering to keep playtesting and refining the game further.

### Worldbuilding: The Dark Ages

An unimaginative working title, I want to create my own take of the "dark ages" before the rise of the Frankish empire. Think 450s AD and beyond, mixing history with fantasy. This will need a lot of research, so it's little more than a raw idea as of now.

When the Yvari RPG system develops well, this could be a second setting adapting this system and making use of it.

### Worldbuilding: Iron Butterfly

A dystopian world - demons and cyberpunk, at the moment this is more a conglomerate of styles than anything else. I'm very curious where this will lead me (and if I can make it work at all).
