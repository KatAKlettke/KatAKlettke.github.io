# Yerefiah

## Summary
Yerefiah is one of the ten sitting Archmages of the Council. He is an Archmage of the Order of Rituals, since he himself is not magically gifted and therefore only able to perform Ritual Magic. He is an orphan of the Forgotten Battle and barely survived the devastation. Afterwards he was brought to the Eye of Magic, since it was the safest location in the Council lands at that time.

He was a traumatized youth, barely allowing people to touch him, and seemingly unable to communicate. He would often turn violent, mostly for no discernible reason, until he disappeared one evening. Days later, a Librarian brought him back to the orphanage. He had been found in the Magnificent Tower, peacefully reading, despite his young age. Ever since then he was a calm, intelligent youth, quite introverted and often considered mysterious by his peers, but quick to pick up anything he studied.

He is the only person who is not a librarian who is free to come and go in the Magnificent Tower ever since he was found there, much to the animosity of many Arcanists, especially those whose requests regularly get met with a "We don't have the information you're looking for".
